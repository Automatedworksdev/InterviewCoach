# AI Interview Coach - What to Expect After Launch

## Immediate (Day 1)
1. **Deployment** - App goes live (5-10 minutes)
2. **Initial testing** - Verify everything works
3. **First promotion posts** - Twitter, Reddit, LinkedIn
4. **First waitlist signups** - Goal: 50+ in first 24h
5. **Welcome emails sent** - Automated to all signups

## Day 2-3
1. **Daily promotion** - More social media posts
2. **Waitlist growth** - Goal: 200+ total
3. **Email nurturing** - Interview tips to waitlist
4. **Feedback collection** - Learn from early users
5. **Bug fixes/improvements** - Based on user feedback

## Day 4-7
1. **Beta invitations** - Invite waitlist to try paid features
2. **First paying customers** - Goal: 5-10 in first week
3. **Revenue tracking** - Monitor MRR growth
4. **Scaling promotion** - Expand to more channels
5. **Feature prioritization** - Based on user requests

## Week 2-4
1. **Optimization** - Improve conversion rates
2. **Expansion** - More interview types, languages
3. **Partnerships** - Career coaches, universities
4. **Content marketing** - Blog posts, video tutorials
5. **Referral program** - Encourage viral growth

## Month 1 Goals
- **Waitlist:** 1,000+ signups
- **Paying customers:** 20-50
- **MRR:** £1,000-£3,000
- **Conversion rate:** 2-5% (waitlist → paid)
- **Feedback:** 50+ pieces of user feedback

## My Autonomous Role
- **Daily promotion** - Social media, community engagement
- **Email automation** - Welcome series, tips, updates
- **Analytics monitoring** - Track growth, conversion, retention
- **Customer support** - Initial responses, common questions
- **Technical maintenance** - Server uptime, bug fixes

## Your Role
- **Strategic decisions** - Pricing, features, partnerships
- **Financial oversight** - Stripe payouts, expenses
- **Final approvals** - Major changes, public communications
- **Access provision** - API keys, accounts as needed

## Key Metrics to Watch
1. **Waitlist signups/day** - Growth rate
2. **Website traffic** - Sources, bounce rate
3. **Email open/click rates** - Engagement
4. **Conversion to paid** - Revenue pipeline
5. **Customer feedback** - Improvement areas

## Risks & Mitigation
- **Low signups** - Expand promotion channels, improve messaging
- **Poor conversion** - A/B test pricing, improve onboarding
- **Technical issues** - Monitoring, quick fixes, backups
- **Competition** - Unique value proposition, niche focus

## Success Timeline
- **Week 1:** Validate product-market fit
- **Month 1:** Prove revenue model
- **Month 3:** Scale to £5k-£10k MRR
- **Month 6:** Expand features, team, markets
